GitForge Word Counter

Your favorite Word Counter, with a dark mode!

GitForge Word Counter is a free online tool to count the number of characters, words, and paragraphs in your text. GitForge Word Counter can also count the number of digits, spaces, and special characters. Make sure you have the required number of words for your essay or article by counting them instantly.

Also, GitForge Word Counter has other themes in the settings, so check them out if you want a different look. Please bookmark this page to use GitForge Word Counter whenever you need.
